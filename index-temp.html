About Six Kingdoms

Six Kingdoms is a landscape design company based in South Africa's Garden Route. We specialize in creating sustainable, ecologically-sound landscapes that work in harmony with nature.

Our approach combines ecological design principles to create beautiful, productive spaces that enhance biodiversity, restore ecosystems, and provide lasting value for our clients.
