What is an EcoPool?

Chlorine-free, living water that keeps itself clean — filtered by an ecosystem of aquatic plants and beneficial micro-organisms, the way water works in any healthy waterway.

Natural swimming pools work with nature's genius to create clean, clear, living water. Our custom, site specific, designs ensure the highest quality, naturally filtered water for you and your family. Our partnership with EcoPools Africa ensures industry leading approaches to designing, installing and maintaining innovative natural pool solutions. From water features and naturalistic ponds, to crystal clear modern pools with a natural twist, our techniques and methods can ensure healthly, living water for you and your loved ones.

Bring living water into your home with our tried and tested systems. Nature is the ultimate luxury.

About Six Kingdoms

We Build Pools That Belong Here

Six Kingdoms is an ecological design and natural pool practice based on South Africa’s Garden Route. For over a decade, we’ve been designing EcoPools, restoring fynbos, and helping landowners understand and work with their land — rather than against it.

Every pool we build starts with your site. The slope, the soil, the local aquatic species, how the seasons move through your land — all of that shapes the design before we put a spade in the ground.

Follow our work @six_kingdoms Facebook

Why Choose a Natural Pool?

Healthier Swimming

No chlorine burn, no red eyes, no chemical residue on your skin. Just water that's genuinely soft to swim in.

Chlorine-Free

No chlorine, no algaecides, no hidden costs of chemical management. Biology does the work instead.

Biodiversity

Dragonflies, kingfishers, tree frogs — within a season, the pool becomes an actual habitat. That's not a side effect, it's part of the design.
